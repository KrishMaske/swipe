# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Overview
Student Dev (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates a financial application that allows users to connect their financial accounts and view transaction data for personal finance insights. We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal and financial data responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Email address and authentication information used to create and manage your account
- User preferences and settings

### Financial Data (via Plaid)
When you connect a financial account, we receive data from Plaid, Inc. including:
- Account information (institution name, account type)
- Transaction data (merchant name, amount, date, category)
- Account balances

We **do not** receive or store your bank login credentials.

---

## How We Use Information
We use collected data to:
- Provide core application functionality (account linking, transaction history, analytics)
- Generate insights, summaries, or categorization of transactions
- Improve reliability, security, and performance of the application

---

## Data Storage and Security
We implement industry-standard security practices, including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Encrypted storage of Plaid access tokens
- Role-based access control and database row-level security
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access

Access to user data is restricted to authorized systems only.

---

## Data Sharing
We only share data with trusted third parties necessary to operate the service:
- **Plaid, Inc.** for financial data connectivity
- Cloud infrastructure and hosting providers
- Authentication and database providers

We do **not** sell user data.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion
We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, stored financial and personal data will be permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

---

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Disconnect financial accounts at any time

---

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
